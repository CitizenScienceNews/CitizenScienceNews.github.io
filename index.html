About Us

CitizenScienceNews.com is a crowdsourced and curated news site dedicated to Citizen Science. Here at CitizenScienceNews.com we seek to bring you the latest and most important trending topics in the field. From publications to events, policy and projects, with a little of everything in between. If its news, and its #CitSci, then its here.
